Hui GAO (高辉)


I am an associate professor in SUSTech (in Shenzhen, China).
Currently we have Yong Hu and Yannan Qiu in the number theory group.
See our Number Theory seminar.
The department constantly have postdoc positions available (on mathjobs.org).
Feel free to contact me if you work in related areas.

Research interests: Number theory and arithmetic geometry.
In particular: p-adic Hodge theory and p-adic Langlands program.
